About Us

Legacy Web Agencies is a student-led web and business solutions studio based at Maseno University. We combine creativity, technology, and practical finance knowledge to help young entrepreneurs succeed.

Our Mission

To equip students and small businesses with digital tools and business insights that are easy to use, effective, and affordable.

What We Do

  • Design fast, responsive websites tailored to small budgets
  • Implement affiliate monetization strategies (Jumia, Amazon, etc.)
  • Provide practical finance and business coaching for students
  • Offer ongoing local support through WhatsApp & M-Pesa

Our Process

  1. Initial consultation & project briefing
  2. Design mockups & client approval
  3. Development, testing, and quality assurance
  4. Project handover with payment setup and training
