About Us

Legacy Web Agencies is a student‑led web and business solutions studio based around Maseno, Kenya. We help students, entrepreneurs and microbusinesses build affordable, mobile‑friendly websites and monetization strategies — including affiliate setups and simple finance coaching.

Our Mission

To empower students and small businesses with digital tools that are affordable, easy to manage and designed to generate income.

What we do

  • Build fast, responsive websites tailored to small budgets
  • Set up affiliate monetization (Jumia, Amazon etc.) for passive income
  • Offer simple finance & business coaching for student entrepreneurs
  • Provide ongoing local support (WhatsApp & M‑Pesa compatible)

  1. Discovery call & project brief
  2. Design mockup & approval
  3. Development & testing
  4. Handover, payments setup & training
